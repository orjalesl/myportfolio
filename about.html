I'm a data scientist with a passion for turning complex, messy problems into clear, actionable decisions. Whether I'm building a predictive model or designing an automation system, I'm energized by work that moves organizations forward.

I started my career in data analytics and machine learning, where I developed a strong foundation in MLOps and building systems that put models into production. I'm looking to bring these skills in-house, embedding within an organization where I can focus on long-term impact, build on institutional knowledge, and help shape how data drives decisions from the inside.
